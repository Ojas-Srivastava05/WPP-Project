CHRD (Centre for Human Resource Development) is a student chapter that inculcates aesthetic values in the students and creates human resources. We, in CHRD, curate every kind of talent and believe in the overall development of an individual, including cultural aspects. CHRD aims to nurture the talent of students interested in arts, dramatics, music, dance, photography, movie-making & designing. We provide a platform to discuss and develop their creative ideas and, in turn, their personality.
